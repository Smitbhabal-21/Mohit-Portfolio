About Me

Bridging Mumbai's
Past & Future

I am Mohit Manoj Pandarkame, a final-year architecture student at Rachana Sansad's Academy of Architecture — one of India's most celebrated architecture schools, right in the heart of Mumbai.

My work is shaped by Mumbai itself: the tension between its Victorian Gothic heritage and glass-and-steel ambition, between dense chawls and luxury high-rises, between old Bombay and the new financial capital of India.

As Student Council Treasurer, I've led initiatives bridging design thinking with community impact — the same lens I bring to every project.
