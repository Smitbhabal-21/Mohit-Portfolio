About

Architecture
is the will of an
epoch translated
into space.

I am a final-year architecture student at Rachana Sansad's Academy of Architecture, Mumbai — one of India's most prestigious architecture schools. My work sits at the intersection of contemporary form-making and contextual sensitivity.

As Student Council Treasurer, I have developed not just design acumen but leadership and financial stewardship. I believe architecture must speak to its users, its climate, its history.

From luxury towers in Bandra to heritage restoration in Fort district, from Dharavi's complex urban fabric to intimate penthouse interiors — each project is a unique conversation with place.

Expertise

What I Do

Designing contemporary living spaces that balance luxury, sustainability, and the unique demands of Mumbai's high-density urban environment. From high-rise towers to boutique residential complexes, I create spaces that enhance quality of life.

  • AutoCAD
  • Revit/BIM
  • SketchUp
  • Lumion
  • 3D Visualization

Mumbai's colonial and vernacular heritage is under constant pressure from development. I specialize in adaptive reuse and sensitive restoration — breathing new life into historic structures while preserving their cultural memory and architectural integrity.

  • Heritage Documentation
  • Conservation Principles
  • Adaptive Reuse
  • Structural Analysis

Cities are living organisms. My urban planning work focuses on equitable, people-centred design — from large-scale masterplans like Dharavi to micro-interventions in Mumbai's complex chawl fabric. I believe in design that uplifts communities.

  • Urban Design
  • GIS Mapping
  • Masterplanning
  • Community Engagement

The interior is where architecture becomes intimate. I craft interior environments that are both spatially generous and materially rich — balancing natural light, material texture, and spatial flow to create homes and workspaces that resonate.

  • Interior Design
  • Material Boards
  • 3ds Max
  • V-Ray
  • Enscape
